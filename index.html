Design principles

Built around real play, not decoration.

01

One job per addon

Each addon has a narrow purpose, clear settings, and avoids overlapping roles with existing tools.

02

Competitive readability

Visual decisions prioritize scan speed, non-overlap, and information that matters during combat.

03

Transparent limits

ESO API limits are documented so experimental ideas stay honest and maintainable.
